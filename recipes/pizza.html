Buffalo Chicken Pizza!

Description:

There are so many different specialty pizza options out there, and Buffalo Chicken Pizza just so happens to be my wife's all-time favorite! So it was pretty important for me to figure out how to make the BEST version of this at home. This recipe has the best balance of tangy buffalo sauce, cool and creamy blue cheese, and even a few colorful add-ons like red onions, banana peppers, and green onions! This recipe keeps these flavors in perfect balance, it's a must-try for your next pizza night!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups shredded chicken
  • 1/3 cup buffalo sauce, plus more to drizzle on top
  • ¼ cup blue cheese dressing, divided. Can sub Ranch.
  • 1 lb. pizza dough, at room temp
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup red onions, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup banana pepper rings, patted dry

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450° F. Note: If you have a pizza stone, put it in the oven while it preheats for more even cooking and a browner, crisper crust.
  2. Combine the shredded chicken, buffalo sauce, and 1 tablespoon of blue cheese. Let the chicken marinate while you prepare the remaining ingredients.
  3. Spread the dough onto a lightly greased, 12-inch pizza pan (or stone). Poke holes in it with a fork to prevent the crust from bubbling up in the oven.
  4. Top with pizza sauce, then with most of the mozzarella. Add the chicken, onions, and banana peppers, then the remaining mozzarella.
  5. Drizzle with 3 tablespoons of blue cheese, along with 1-2 tablespoons of buffalo sauce.
  6. Bake for 13 minutes and remove from the oven.
  7. Combine the melted butter, Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder. Brush it over the crust of the pizza and return it to the oven.
  8. Bake for 2-3 more minutes, or until the crust begins to brown.
  9. Garnish with green onions, red pepper flakes, and parsley. Slice and serve!
